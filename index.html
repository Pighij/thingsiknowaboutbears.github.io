What is bears?

Bears is an mammal, bears care all their family members, and there are many types of bears. The polar bear is the biggest bear, and the sun bear is the smallest. Most bears can also stand with only two feet - like us!

Bear's diet

Bears are omnivores, which means they both eat other animals and plants. Bears eat berries, birds, other mammals, insects, and fish like salmons.

Habitat

Bears lives in many habitats, including forests, mountains, deserts and grassy areas, and so on.

Glossary

Mammal - an animal with a backbone
Omnivores - an animal that BOTH eat orther animals and plants
Habitats - a natural place where animals live
